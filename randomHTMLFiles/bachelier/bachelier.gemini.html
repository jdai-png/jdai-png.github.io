Option Pricing Distribution Visualizer

This visualization compares two historical models for pricing options: the **Bachelier model** (assuming a normal distribution of stock prices) and the **Black-Scholes model** (assuming a lognormal distribution). It also illustrates the payoff of a call option at a specified strike price.
